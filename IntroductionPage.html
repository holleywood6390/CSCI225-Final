Math Quiz Introductory Page

How to Start:

To begin one of the quizzes below, click the "Begin" button located in thhe middle of the quiz box for that quiz. Clicking the "begin" button will take you to a new page upon which the current leaderboard for that quiz will be displayed as well as a "Start Quiz" button. Each quiz will consist of ten fill in the blank questions. Each quiz will have three difficulty levels to test the extent of your knowledge along with a final bonus question. The final mixed quiz will have questions from each quiz to fully test your knowledge. At the end of your quiz you will recieve your score and see the top 3 players for that quiz.
